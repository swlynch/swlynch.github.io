Steve Lynch

Junior Developer


Ever since my dad, an old-school programmer, handed me a BASIC programming book when I was nine years-old, I have always seen code as a way of ordering and making sense of world. Even through my tens years of public school teaching as a middle school English teacher, I worked with students on developing the thinking and logic skills used in coding, all the while developing my own skills with languages and interface like Ruby, Python, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi.

I am currently in the Java cohort at Tech Elevator in Columbus as my way to fully immerse myself in technology and to make that change to become a full-time developer upon graduation. I am looking to continue my work in shaping the world using code and data, regardless of the organization I work for.

I get excited about the possibilities of using technology and code to support better and stronger human interactions and working for companies that integrate mindfulness into their processes and products.
